Welcome! This tool generates AI-powered equity research briefs — similar to what analysts at investment banks produce.

How to use: Type any stock ticker (like AAPL for Apple, or RELIANCE for Reliance Industries) and click Generate. The AI will produce a structured research brief covering company overview, financial metrics, catalysts, risks, and an investment thesis.

Single vs Compare: Use Single Stock for a deep-dive on one company. Use Compare Stocks to see two companies side by side — great for understanding relative strengths.

Glossary:
• P/E Ratio — Price-to-Earnings. How much investors pay per dollar of earnings. Lower = cheaper.
• Market Cap — Total value of all shares. Tells you how big the company is.
• ROE — Return on Equity. How efficiently the company uses shareholder money. Higher = better.
• Bull Case — Reasons the stock could go UP.
• Bear Case — Reasons the stock could go DOWN.
• Outlook — The AI's overall assessment: Bullish (positive), Neutral, or Bearish (negative).
